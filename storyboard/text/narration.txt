Deep in an underground dungeon where the dead can be revived, four unlikely adventurers push toward the final depths — each chasing a very different goal. 
They help each other and fight together.
A monk who walked out of his temple. A wizard chasing specimens. A ninja hunting for medicine. And a very small, very hungry, very dangerous mascot.
After surviving the worst the dungeon had thrown at them, the team finally slept. They deserved it.
They did not deserve what came next.
Hungry, angry, and now deeply suspicious of their own mascot — the team pushed on anyway. Because the dungeon's final chamber was close. And something ancient was breathing on the other side of the door.
