## Story Context / 故事背景

*Deep in an underground dungeon where the dead can be revived, four unlikely adventurers push toward the final depths — each chasing a very different goal. Their mascot, the tiny 42cm dog-bear Mr. Star, is not what he seems. After a hard-fought journey, the team faces the dungeon's first great boss: the Dragon. But something far more dangerous has been riding in Enos's backpack all along.*

*在这座死者可以复活的地下迷城深处，四位奇特的冒险者各怀目的，向着最深处进发。他们的吉祥物——42厘米高的小狗熊Mr.Star，绝不是看起来那么无害。经过艰苦的旅程，队伍面临地下城的第一个大Boss：恶龙。但真正危险的东西，一直藏在Enos的背包里。*
